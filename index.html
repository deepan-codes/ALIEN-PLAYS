    🕹️ HOW TO PLAY

  1. Player 1 starts.
  2. Click "Roll Dice" 🎲 — the number you roll (except 1) is added to your Current Score.
  3. If you roll a 1, your Current Score becomes 0, and the turn switches to the next player.
  4. To avoid risk, click "Hold" — your current Score added to your Total Score.
  5. which player to reach 100 Total Score and click "Hold" wins the game! 🏆
